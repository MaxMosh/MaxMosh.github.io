ÉTUDES

After two years of CPGE (maths and physics) at Lycée Michelet in Vanves, near Paris, my results in the competitive entrance exam allowed me to enter Grenoble INP - Phelma, a graduate school of engineering in materials. At the end of my first year, I realized that I wasn't cut out for physics, and after much thought, I decided to leave Grenoble for Toulouse, and start a new engineering program in the digital sciences department.

RÉSEAU

It's well known that each engineer's career is different, and I got a very good feeling of that from him. He also told me about the importance of the English language in the professional world.
